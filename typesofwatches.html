Types of watches

There are many kinds of watches, and even more sub-categories. The list would inmensly big if they were all listed here. So I'll go over all the main categories with some explanation on how they work, so you might get an idea about what you like more. Also don't forget that every kind has an average price, but the prices still always range from cheap to expensive. So a normally cheap type can have a some expensive units in it.


Mechanical watches

As mentioned before mechanical watches are the oldest type. They are now the hardest(and the most expensive) to create. They're even not as accurate as other kinds are. But still they are by far the most expensive ones. people feel that these are 'real', watches and are willing to pay thousands of dollars for them.

Electric watches

These watches are very uncommen. Not because they're really expensive, but because the 'quartz watch, which I'll talk about in a bit, just almost completly overshadows it, as previously mentioned. Maybe it is better to just a quartz watch. Still, if you like different mechanics used in this type, go for it, they are not bad, far from that actually, there are just better similair options.

Quartz watches

Quartz watches are currently the most commen. With good reason, they're easiest to make. Factories can pump these things out like if it was nothing. They're also very cheap to make. To top that off, quartz watches are also the most precise watches of them all(except for atomic watches, but you'll probably barely ever see them). So these watches are the most accessible for a normal fellow. If you just want a watch for its functionality or if your budget isn't that big, this watch is perfect for you.

Digital watches

Digital watches are pretty new and give us a fresh new option for our choosing. Digital watches can range from just a digital clock to watches with a touch screen and all new options that other portable devices also have, like being able to play music. If you don't like the class or if you're interested in the new options made avaible for them or if you just want the newest thing, these fresh guys are for you.
