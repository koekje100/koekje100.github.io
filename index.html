Hi, this is a website about watches. I always watch if someone wears a watch. Aside from being very nice to wear, they also give a person a lot of style. I don't know why, but whenever someone wears one, I instantly like them more. There is just something about watches that gives a person a nice look,
especially when someone wears them with a suit or blouse.
It is a really fun thing to match your watch with your suit and
to see everything just fitting together.

To be completly honest, I actually didn't give a dime about
watches when I was younger. Never even noticed the things.
But that changed big time when I needed a watch I started to
go to high school. I needed a way to check the time to see if I
was going fast enough.

So I asked my mom if she could get me one. At that age, I didn't really think about how expensive a watch could be, nor would I care back then.
So she gave a random black cheap watch. It doesn't look special or has a lot of style, but after all these years I still wear my faithful companion almost every day. The wrist strap is now almost broken, and I'll take that a sign for when I'm gonna get a new one. Not that I'll throw my first buddy in the garbage bin, he'll always have a special place in my heart and will never be forgotten.
